== Description ==

Apex Market is a feature-rich WordPress theme designed for eCommerce businesses, offering a modern, responsive design optimized for performance on all devices. Its easy customization options, including a powerful theme customizer and drag-and-drop page builder, allow users to personalize their site effortlessly. With full WooCommerce integration, Apex Market supports advanced product filtering, multiple header and footer styles, and is SEO optimized to enhance search engine rankings. Additionally, the theme's compatibility with multi-vendor marketplace plugins like Dokan and WC Vendors makes it ideal for businesses looking to manage multiple sellers.Furthermore, Apex Market ensures fast loading speeds and includes extensive documentation and dedicated support, making it accessible for users of all skill levels. Regular updates provide new features, improvements, and security enhancements, ensuring your site remains competitive. Whether you are setting up an online store, a multi-vendor marketplace, or a boutique shop, Apex Market delivers the flexibility, functionality, and aesthetic appeal needed to create a professional and effective eCommerce website.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
